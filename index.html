About Me

All my life I have had an interest for creation, how things work, and serving people in a meaningful way. I served in the Army for 3 years, and upon leaving the military I landed an apprenticeship with a company that paid for me to go to school for a year to earn a certificate in Diesel Technology. The program exposed me to electrical systems, PLC logic, basic refrigeration, and diesel engines. After completing of program I worked for the company traveling for 3 years working on generators, electrical distribution, HVAC systems, and other equipment. After having kids I decided to settle down working for a manufacturer for HVAC equipment. I work as a test stand technician working to find faults in systems and make repairs as needed. My mind has been eager to continue growth and building knowledge. Coding has given me a way to expand my knowledge and allow me to create in a whole new way. The Technology industry's constant change and improvement is exciting to me. I enjoy learning new things and look forward to my future in Tech.
